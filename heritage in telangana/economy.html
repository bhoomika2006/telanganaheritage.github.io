Economy of Telangana




Telangana is one of the fastest growing states in India posing average annual growth rate of 13.90% over the last five years. Telangana's nominal gross state domestic product for the year 2020-21 stands at ₹11.05 lakh crore. Service sector is the largest contributor to the Telangana's economy with a share of about 65% in the year 2018-19. Growth in services has largely been fuelled by IT services with the State holding leading position in IT & ITeS in the country in terms of production and exports.

As of March 2020, total installed power capacity of Telangana was 16,394.43 MW. Out of this, 7,953.65 MW was contributed by state utilities, 6,295.28 MW by private utilities and 2,145.50 MW by central utilities. Thermal power was the largest contributor to the state’s total installed capacity with 9,640.54 MW, followed by hydropower at 2,479.93 MW, renewable energy at 4,125.23 MW and nuclear power at 148.73 MW. State has the highest installed solar capacity of 3,621 MW in the country and plans to install 5000 MW in 2020-21.
